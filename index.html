Pizza

My family has pizza night where we have pizza for dinner on Sundays most weeks. One week most of the family was out of town and my sister and I made our own.

Halloween Rice Krispies and Cookies

I thought it would be fun to make some little rice krispies and make them look like ghosts, so I dipped these in white chocolate and then put some black icing eyes on them. Then I thought that it would match the color scheme of Halloween to have some kind of dark cookie to go with them. These ones are supposed to be a copycat recipe of a Crumbl cookie.
